About UniNest

Student productivity web app built for offline-first study life.

Design Thinking & Idea Lab

Project overview

Subject: Design Thinking & Idea Lab (DT&IL)

College: GH Raisoni College of Engineering and Management, Pune

Branch: Computer Engineering • Division: A • Year: First Year (FY) • AY: 2025–26

UniNest is a lightweight, offline-capable student productivity web app to organize tasks, take notes, store study resources, and track progress with a simple stress hint.

Key features

  • Task management with completion tracking
  • Notes storage system
  • Study resource organizer
  • Progress tracking dashboard
  • Workload-based stress indicator
  • Offline functionality using LocalStorage
  • Portable and easy to use on any system
